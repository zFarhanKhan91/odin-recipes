Lassi(Buttermilk)

Lassi is a traditional yogurt-based drink originating from the Indian subcontinent, prepared by blending yogurt with water and various flavorings until frothy. It is primarily served chilled and comes in two main styles: a sweet version often infused with sugar, rosewater, or fruit (like mango), and a savory version seasoned with salt and roasted cumin. Beyond its popularity as a refreshing summer beverage, lassi is valued in Ayurvedic traditions for its cooling properties and its ability to aid digestion through natural probiotics.

Ingredients

  • Yogurt(curd)
  • Water or milk
  • Sugar or salt
  • Cardamom (optional)

Steps

  1. Add yogurt to a bowl or blender.
  2. Add water or milk and mix well.
  3. Add sugar or salt as needed.
  4. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  5. Serve chilled.
